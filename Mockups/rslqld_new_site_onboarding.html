Floor Structure
Define the floors and levels of this site

Add each floor or zone that will be inspected. Drag to reorder. You can always add or remove floors later via the checklist editor.

💡Use short IDs for the floor tab (e.g. GF, L1, B2, Roof, Ext). The full name is shown in the checklist.
✓For a simple single-floor site (e.g. a Bundall suite), just add "GF — Ground Floor" and you're done.
📋
Checklist Setup
Upload a floor plan or manually define areas and items

Choose your preferred setup method. You can mix both — upload a plan for layout reference and manually name the areas.

📐
Upload floor plan
Photo or scan — phone camera fine. PNG, JPG, or PDF. Must show room layout and labels.

✓ Floor plan uploaded — areas will be auto-generated as placeholders

💡Areas will be created with placeholder names (Office 01, Meeting Room A etc.). Inspectors will be prompted to confirm the real names on their first visit.
Areas & Items
⚠️
Use Placeholder area for spaces you can't name from the floor plan (inspector names them on first visit). Use Named area for spaces you know (e.g. Toilets, Kitchen, Carpark).
